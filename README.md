# The Last Revelation – Library Escape Room

## 🎮 Game Concept

"The Last Revelation" is a first-person puzzle escape room game set in a mysterious, forgotten library. You find yourself trapped in a single room, surrounded by misplaced objects and cryptic environmental clues. Your mission is to restore order to the library by solving a series of puzzles. With each puzzle you solve, you get one step closer to unlocking the exit and securing your freedom.

A ticking clock adds to the suspense. If you run out of time, the room resets, and you'll have to start over.

## 🧩 Puzzles

The game features five interactive puzzles:

*   **📄 Paper Disposal:** Find and place scattered papers into the correct bin.
*   **📚 Book Arrangement:** Return misplaced books to their rightful shelves.
*   **💡 Lamp Placement:** Place the lamp back on its designated desk.
*   **🕯️ Chandelier Placement:** Reattach the fallen chandelier to its proper place on the ceiling.
*   **📖 Object Organization:** Arrange various decorative and library objects (like candles, chairs, and other props) in their correct locations.

Completing all these tasks is the key to unlocking the exit.

## 🕹️ Controls

*   **Movement:**
    *   `WASD` to move around the room.
    *   `Mouse` to look around.
*   **Interaction:**
    *   `E` to pick up and place objects.

## 🏆 Winning and Losing

*   **Win Condition:** To win, you must:
    1.  Complete all 5 puzzle tasks.
    2.  Unlock and open the exit door.
    3.  Escape the room to see the win screen.

*   **Lose Condition:** You will lose if:
    *   The timer reaches zero.
    *   The "Game Over" screen will appear, and the scene will automatically reset.

## 🚀 How to Play

1.  Click **Play** on the start menu to begin.
2.  Explore the room and interact with objects to solve the puzzles.
3.  Complete all puzzles before the timer runs out to unlock the exit.
4.  Walk through the open door to escape.
